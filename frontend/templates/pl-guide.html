Welcome to the Help Page

Welcome to the game where your skills get tested! Guide your character through a maze of floating lines and obstacles without touching them. Use your cursor to move with precision and avoid the spinning and moving obstacles in your path. Your goal is to survive as long as possible. The longer you play, the tougher the challenge gets, with more complex patterns and obstacles. Every successful dodge earns you points, and the longer you last, the higher your score. Ready for this adrenaline-fueled adventure? The challenge awaits!
