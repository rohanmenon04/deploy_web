Welcome to the Help Page

Welcome to the game where your skills are put to the test! Your mission is to guide your character through a maze of floating lines and obstacles without making contact. Using your cursor, maneuver your character with precision and agility to avoid colliding with the spinning and moving obstacles that cross your path. As you navigate through the maze, your goal is to survive for as long as possible. With each passing moment, the intensity of the challenge increases, presenting you with ever more intricate patterns and obstacles to overcome. Every successful dodge earns you points, and the longer you manage to stay afloat amidst the chaos, the higher your score will soar. But be warned, the path ahead is fraught with danger, and the slightest misstep could spell the end of your journey. Stay sharp, stay focused, and may your reflexes be swift as you strive to achieve the highest score possible. Are you ready to embark on this adrenaline-fueled adventure? The challenge awaits!

Lines is a game designed by our enthusiastic team, made for the purpose of pure entertainment. The game specifically was developed by Lucas Masclee, a young, up and coming Unity game developer. The team then developed it into a web application so that you can enjoy the game on your favourite browser anywhere in the world.
